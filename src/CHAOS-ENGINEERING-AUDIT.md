| 5️⃣ | **iOS Tab Suspension** — Lock screen mid-tremor-test, unlock after 5 min | **`motion-sensor.service.ts`** L370–409 (`attachTremorListener`), L132-144 | 🔴 **Critical** | iOS pauses `devicemotion` events when the screen is locked. After 5 minutes locked: (a) The countdown has likely already expired, calling `stopTremorTest()` (L456) while the `devicemotion` listener is still attached.
